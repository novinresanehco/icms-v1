## II. CRITICAL SUCCESS METRICS

- Authentication: Multi-factor mandatory
- Authorization: Role-based, granular
- Encryption: AES-256 minimum
- SSL: TLS 1.3 required
- Sessions: Secure, time-limited
- Input: All validated
